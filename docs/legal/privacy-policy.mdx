## 1. Introduction and Scope

### 1.1 Our Commitment to Privacy

At Weblinq, we are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your personal data with transparency and respect. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use our Services.

### 1.2 Who We Are

**Weblinq** is a cloud-based API platform providing web scraping, browser automation, and data extraction services. We act as both a data controller and data processor depending on the context of data handling.

<Info>
**Data Controller:** When we collect and process your account information, usage data, and billing details.

**Data Processor:** When we process data on your behalf through our scraping and automation services.

</Info>

### 1.3 Scope of This Policy

This Privacy Policy applies to:

- Our website (weblinq.dev)
- The Weblinq API platform and services
- Customer support interactions
- Marketing communications
- Any other services we provide

### 6.2 GDPR Rights (EU/EEA Residents)

<Warning>
  If you are located in the European Union or European Economic Area, you have additional rights under the General Data
  Protection Regulation (GDPR).
</Warning>

**Enhanced GDPR Rights:**

- **Right to Rectification:** Correct inaccurate personal data
- **Right to Erasure:** Request deletion of personal data ("right to be forgotten")
- **Right to Restrict Processing:** Limit how we process your data
- **Right to Object:** Object to processing based on legitimate interests
- **Right to Data Portability:** Receive your data and transfer it to another service
- **Right to Withdraw Consent:** Withdraw consent for processing at any time
- **Right to Lodge Complaints:** File complaints with your local data protection authority

### 6.3 CCPA Rights (California Residents)

**California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) Rights:**

- **Right to Know:** Categories and specific personal information we collect
- **Right to Delete:** Request deletion of personal information
- **Right to Opt-Out:** Opt out of the "sale" of personal information (we don't sell data)
- **Right to Non-Discrimination:** Equal service regardless of privacy choices
